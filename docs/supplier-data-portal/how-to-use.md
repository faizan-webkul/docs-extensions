# UnoPim Supplier Data Portal

The **UnoPim Supplier Data Portal** is a dedicated space where your suppliers can log in and submit product data directly into UnoPim — without needing access to your full admin panel.

Instead of collecting product information manually through spreadsheets or emails, suppliers upload their own products through a separate portal. Your team then reviews and approves that data before it becomes part of your official catalog.

---

## How It Works

The portal involves two sides working together:

**Admin / Supplier Handler (your team)**
- Creates supplier accounts and manages access
- Reviews products submitted by suppliers
- Approves, rejects, or requests changes before anything enters the catalog

**Supplier**
- Logs in to their own dedicated portal
- Creates or imports products for review
- Updates rejected products and resubmits them

---

## The Product Lifecycle

```
Supplier submits product
        ↓
Product enters Review stage
        ↓
Handler reviews the product
        ↓
Approved → appears in UnoPim Catalog
Rejected → supplier sees comments and can resubmit
```
