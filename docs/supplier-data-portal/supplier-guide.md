# Supplier Guide

Suppliers access the portal through a dedicated URL, separate from the Unopim admin panel.

## Logging in

Go to `http://your-domain.com/supplier/login` and enter the credentials provided by your admin. If you forget your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page.

---

## Dashboard

After logging in, the dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your product activity:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Products** | All products you have submitted |
| **Pending** | Submitted but not yet reviewed by the admin |
| **Approved** | Accepted and added to the catalog |
| **Rejected** | Returned by the admin — check the reason and resubmit if needed |

---

## Managing Products

Navigate to **Products** in the sidebar to view and manage your product listings.

### Adding a product

1. Click **Create Product**.
2. Select a product type (simple, configurable, etc.) and confirm.
3. Fill in all required attributes — the fields shown depend on the attribute family assigned to you by the admin.
4. Upload product images where prompted.
5. Click **Save**. The product is submitted with **Pending** status and queued for admin review.

### Editing a product

Click **Edit** on any product in the list to update its details. You can edit a product at any time before it is approved.

### Copying a product

Use the **Copy** action to duplicate an existing product as a starting point for a new one.

### Deleting a product

Click **Delete** on a product to remove it permanently. Mass delete is also available by selecting multiple rows.

---

## Import & Export

### Importing products

Use **Settings → Import** to upload a CSV or ZIP file containing multiple products at once.

1. Create a new import, select your file, and save.
2. Click **Validate** to check for errors before committing.
3. Click **Import Now** to start the job. Track progress in the **Tracker** tab.
4. Download the error report if any rows failed.

Download a sample CSV or ZIP from the import page to see the expected format.

### Exporting products

Use **Settings → Export** to download your product data.

1. Create a new export configuration and save.
2. Click **Export Now** to generate the file.
3. Download the output file from the export page once the job is complete.

### Tracker

The **Tracker** shows the real-time status of all your import and export jobs — step-by-step progress, logs, and downloadable archives.

---

## Notifications

A notification bell appears in the portal header. You receive a notification whenever an admin **approves** or **rejects** one of your products. Click the notification to go directly to the relevant product.

Use **Mark All as Read** to clear all pending notifications at once.

---

## Account Settings

Click your name in the top-right corner to access account settings. You can update your display name, email, password, preferred locale, and timezone.
